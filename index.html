Privacy Policy for MyBravePath

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

MyBravePath is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed with privacy as a core principle - your data stays on your device.

Data Collection and Storage

MyBravePath does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data entered in the app, including:

  • Brave moments entries
  • Notes and descriptions
  • Emoji selections
  • Date and time information

...is stored locally on your device only using secure local storage.

Data We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Crash reports
  • Any other telemetry data

Third-Party Services

MyBravePath does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

The only external connection made by the app is when you explicitly choose to open the "Ko-fi" support link, which opens in your web browser.

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete individual entries at any time
  • You can delete all data through the app settings
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data

Children's Privacy

MyBravePath does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact me at:

  • Email: kingagjermak@outlook.com
  • Ko-fi: ko-fi.com/kinjer94

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect any data:

  • You have complete control over your data
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No data leaves your device (except when you explicitly choose to open external links)

In short: Your data is yours, stays on your device, and is never sent anywhere.
